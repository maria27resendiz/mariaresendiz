Why Downtown Dallas? Well, here are a few fun facts to get you hooked.

Dallas is the third largest city in Texas! It attracts a lot of visitors, especially during the beginning of fall when the Texas State Fair opens. You can eat al the funnel cakes you please, ride fantastic rides and take picutres with Big Tex.

A lot of celebrities are from Dallas such as Owen Wilson (wow!), Kelly Clarkson, and Tommy Lee Jones!

Did you know that the frozen margarita machine was invented in Dallas?

The Trinity River Corridor Project, when completed, will be more than 10 times the size of New York's Central Park. That's crazy!

Galleria Dallas is home to the country's tallest indoor Christmas tree. The Dallas-Forth Worth metroplex has more shopping centers per capita than any other city in the U.S.

We are known for our strong tec mex dishes. It's a big foodie city with thriving restaurants from all cultures.

Our sports are all big deal, not to mention the Dallas Cowboys.

We have a very inteesting history. Dallas has made history as being the place were the frozen margarita machine was invented, the founding of 7-11 and the creation of the Superbowl. Check out our Old East Dallas.
